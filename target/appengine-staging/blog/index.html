Summer 2020

May 20th, 2020

This summer, outside of Google, I am hoping to:

  1. Explore running (long-distance) and calisthenics
  2. Work on Lambda AI, and formally release the product
  3. Read more (just read "The Happiness Advantage," now I'm reading "How We Learn")

Given that I can't play tennis due to the quarantine, I figured now is a good time to explore other options. I like long-distance running more than sprints, and I usually run for about thirty minutes every morning. I hope to run the equivalent of a half-marathon soon, and run around Mercer Island, which is about 13.5 miles (I did 11 miles earlier in the year).

I am also hoping to explore calisthenics more, especially because we have a pull-up bar. Since this seems easy enough to continue, whether I'm at home or elsewhere, the flexibility lets me continue the routine.

Next, I'm going to continue working on Lambda AI, and release the product and see if we can get any customers. I don't have a strict schedule for this, but we've finished creating the "task widget" that can be embedded in place of ads, and a backend which populates the tasks.

Finally, I want to continue reading, and potentially write down what I learn. I finished reading The Happiness Advantage, but I may re-read it and try to apply the principal concepts in the book.

Thanks for reading the blog! :)
