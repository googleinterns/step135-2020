Projects

Lambda AI

Co-Founder, Dec 2019 - Current

Lambda AI is a crowdsourcing data labeling platform for AI companies. Lambda AI offers an affordable data labeling solution by replacing video ads online with small NLP tasks. This provides AI companies a more cost-effective data labeling service while offering content creators a superior monetization channel.

One for the World: UPenn Chapter

Co-President, May 2020 - Current

One for the World (OFTW) is an organization that is dedicated to ending extreme poverty. OFTW encourages college students to donate 1% of their future salary to the most effective causes at eliminating extreme poverty, and OFTW partners with charity evaluators such as GiveWell to research and identify the charities with the best ROI.

TeamsCode

Co-Founder and Co-President, Jan 2017 - Jul 2019

TeamsCode is a student-run 501(c)(3) nonprofit that encourages high school students to study computer science. TeamsCode hosts amateur programming contests in Washington, Oregon, and California, and offers over one hundred fifty free online problem sets and computer science lessons on teamscode.com.
